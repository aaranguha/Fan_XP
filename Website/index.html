The Process

The Data Behind the Upgrade

A quiet intelligence layer built directly on top of Ticketmaster's live listing data.

01
🎟️1
Pre-Game Snapshot

1 hour before tip-off we capture every active listing on Ticketmaster — building a complete baseline of what's for sale, by section and price.

02
⏱️2
Halftime Scan

At halftime, we scan again. Listings that disappeared without being resold are confirmed no-shows — seats that went dark and never got filled.

03
🪑3
Seat Signal

Teams get a structured feed of confirmed empty seats by section — the intelligence layer that powers halftime upgrade programs and flash sales.
